About the Committee

Shree Shree Ganesh Puja Committee, Punanga began in 2020. With the blessings of Lord Ganesha and with your support, we proudly celebrate our 6th anniversary. Our mission is to organise inclusive , and a meaningful Ganesh Chaturthi celebration for all.
